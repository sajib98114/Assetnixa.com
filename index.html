Digital Asset Strategy for Government Infrastructure Portfolio

Challenge

Solution

TelecomsData Governance

Construction Data Governance for Major Telecoms Rollout

Client: National telecommunications infrastructure program
Challenge

Rapid deployment meant asset data was captured inconsistently across delivery partners. Errors compounded as the program scaled, threatening register integrity at handover.

Solution

A governance framework with data quality rules, partner standards, and validation gates embedded into the delivery workflow.

Outcomes
  • Consistent asset data across all packages
  • Reduced rework in register integration
  • Handover-ready register on program timelines
TransportGIS & Spatial

GIS-Linked Asset Register for a Metropolitan Rail Network

Client: Metropolitan rail infrastructure operator
Challenge

Track, signalling, and structures data sat in separate registers with no spatial link, so maintenance crews couldn't see what assets sat where along the corridor.

Solution

We linked every asset class to a single spatial reference model and surfaced it through GIS dashboards built around real maintenance workflows.

Outcomes
  • Real-time spatial view of the asset network
  • Faster fault location and response
  • Reduced reliance on manual lookups
Utilities & EnergyLifecycle Analytics

Lifecycle Analytics for a Regional Water & Energy Utility

Client: Regional utility asset owner
Challenge

Maintenance was largely reactive, with no clear view of which assets were nearing end of life or driving the most unplanned failures.

Solution

We modelled asset performance and risk across the estate and built lifecycle forecasts to prioritise intervention and capital spend.

Outcomes
  • Optimised maintenance timing
  • Fewer unplanned failures
  • Stronger, evidence-based capital planning
Major Capital ProjectsAsset Information

Asset Information Foundation for a Major Capital Project

Client: Major capital infrastructure delivery program
Challenge

Solution

Outcomes

  • Reduced post-handover remediation
