The business value

A tag is small. Getting it wrong is expensive.

On major infrastructure projects, asset identification is the foundation everything else is built on — the register, maintenance, inspections, GIS and the handover to operations. When tagging is inconsistent or incomplete, the cost compounds into duplicated records, unscannable labels and assets nobody can find.

The Asset Tagging Solution gives delivery and operations teams one consistent, controlled way to identify, code, validate and hand over assets — so the data is right the first time.

  • One enforced tagging standard across every team and discipline
  • Scannable QR / barcode tags linked to the asset record
  • Errors caught at the point of capture, not after handover
  • Clean, structured export that loads into the operator's systems
  • Live coverage and readiness visibility for project leaders
What it does

Everything you need to identify assets — in one place

From the first tag to a handover-ready register, the solution covers the full identification workflow.

Standardised tagging

Apply a consistent asset hierarchy, naming and numbering convention across every package and discipline.

QR & barcode generation

Generate durable, scan-ready labels that link straight back to each asset's record and documents.

Built-in validation

Automatic checks for uniqueness, completeness, format and referential integrity — flagged the moment they occur.

GIS integration

Capture coordinates with every tag so assets are findable in GIS and on the ground, not just in a spreadsheet.

Coverage tracking

Live dashboards show tagging progress, validation status and readiness — by package, system or area.

Handover-ready export

Export clean, structured data mapped to your operator's CMMS, EAM or GIS — ready to load without rework.
